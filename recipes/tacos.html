Steak Tacos!

Description

This easy steak taco recipe is epic! The steak tacos are ridiculously quick and easy, melt-in-your mouth tender and exploding with flavor. The steak is tossed in a sensational spice rub (no marinade required!), pan-seared to juicy perfection, loaded into warmed tortillas then piled high with all your favorite toppings. These steak tacos make the ideal easy weeknight dinner but are also tasty enough to serve guests. The steak is also extremely versatile and can be served in burritos, burrito bowls, salads, etc. and reheats and freezes exceptionally well. Now get ready for the quickest steak tacos of your life!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs flank steak
  • 1 packet of taco seasoning
  • 8 corn tortillas
  • cilantro
  • red onions
  • Cotija cheese
  • sour cream
  • 3 limes
  1. Pat the steak dry with a paper towl and season it with the taco seasoning
  2. Set steak aside for 20-60 minutes to marinate
  3. While steak is marinating dice and chop cilantro and red onions
  4. Half one lime and quarter the others
  5. In a small bowl mixed chopped cliantro, diced onion, the juice of a half of lime, salt and pepper
  6. To cook the steak preheat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in a large cast iron skillet over medium-high heat
  7. Once hot add the steak and let sear for 1-2 to minutes per side being careful not to overcook
  8. Let the steak rest for 5-6 minutes
  9. While the steak is resting, wrap the tortillas in a damp paper towl and heat in the microwave for 60 seconds
  10. For the final step, build your tacos and enjoy!
